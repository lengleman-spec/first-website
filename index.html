Hello! I'm Liz

A freelancer who provides services for digital programming and design content needs

Get to know me

Hi, I'm Liz, a passionate and detail-oriented full stack developer with a love for building clean, functional, and user-friendly digital experiences. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, elegant solutions, and I'm always exploring new tools and technologies to grow my skill set. When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking, swimming, or diving into a good horror novel. I believe in balancing technical skill with curiosity and creativity - and I'm always up for a new challenge.

Education

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree from Kent State University, where I developed a strong foundation in analytical thinking, research, and problem-solving. My education provided both critical thinking skills and hands-on experience, preparing me to approach complex challenges with a practical and thoughtful perspective.
